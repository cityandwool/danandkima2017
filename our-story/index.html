Our Story

Inspired by Legally Blonde, Kima ‘the Great’ Otung became the youngest corporate partner ever at Morrison and Foerster, Los Angeles. Gifted with many talents, the Nigerian-born beauty Queen outsold her longtime friend Beyoncé to become the highest grossing artist ever.

From her first hit, ‘Yasss! Get it Gurl’, to her anthemic, ‘Let me smell your deep love for me’, she fell in love with her drummer while on her seventh world tour, It’s all about me.

After a whirlwind romance, she married her drummer and best friend Dan Ryland in 2017 - voted best looking boy in 2008. Their relationship flourished, taking them from the cutting edge of the London tech and corporate scene to their now family home in Cardiff.

With dreams to build a Ryland island together for the children of Europe, the two never cease to amaze the world with their passion, creativity and love for one another.
